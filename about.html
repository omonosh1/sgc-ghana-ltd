Who are we

In a joint venture with Medline Clearing Agency Limited and Eagle Eye Top Shipping Company Limited, Swift Generation Cargo and Travel Service Limited operates as a licensed freight forwarding business with the goal of providing customers with shipping, clearing, visa, ticketing, and other related services.

Our mission

To offer our customers all necessary import-related services at fair prices with great delivery, as well as to foster employee growth and development to support national development, all while offering dependable, high-quality services to our devoted clients by leveraging our skilled labor force.

Our goal is to provide a secure environment for the export of goods and services from Ghana to other nations worldwide.

Our staff members have received training in cargo packaging, loading, and unloading to guarantee that everything we transport is handled with the utmost care and through effective transportation procedures that guarantee deliveries are delivered securely, undamaged, and quickly.

In order to establish a long-lasting relationship with our clients, we provide them a user-friendly and dependable customer care platform where they can not only track their shipments but also get more information about the packing and shipping options. They would be free to select the option that best suited them.
